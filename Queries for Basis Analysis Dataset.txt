Selecting everything from the data set:
SELECT * FROM "Market Basis Analysis Sheet", LIMIT 5

Viewing the top 5 products:
SELECT "Itemname", SUM (Revenue) AS total_revenue 
FROM "Market Basis Analysis Sheet"
GROUP BY "Itemname"
ORDER BY total_revenue DESC
LIMIT 5

Viewing the top 5 products by country:
SELECT "country", "Itemname", SUM (Revenue) AS total_revenue 
FROM "Market Basis Analysis Sheet"
GROUP BY "Itemname", "country"
ORDER BY "country", total_revenue DESC
LIMIT 5
